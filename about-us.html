OUR

 

GUARD SERVICES

ALPHA Security Services founded in 2020 with initial branches in Kitchener and London, Ontario, was established with a vision to revolutionize the experience of using security guards services. Since then, we have expanded to four branches across Ontario, serving throughout southern Ontario with a diverse clientele of over 450 clients ranging from small businesses to large corporations has earned an excellent reputation for providing vigilant security guards dedicated to protecting people and assets. We prioritize trust, confidentiality, and proficiency in all our projects, offering a custom-tailored suite of best-in-class security services. These include personal security guards for individual protection, event security for managing crowds, mobile patrol services for ongoing surveillance, and loss prevention to safeguard retail and commercial properties.
